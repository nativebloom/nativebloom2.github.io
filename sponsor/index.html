Sponsor a Pollinator Garden

Beautify your land by sponsoring a pollinator garden from the NBI! Our members and volunteers will come to your location and create a native plant garden at no cost. Native plants require very little maintenance, and will help pollinators for long into the future.

How It Works

Whether at your home or at your business, any unused piece of land can help support vital species. By sponsoring a garden, you’re making a positive impact on the local ecosystem. Here's how we work:

  1. Fill out the sponsorship form below to provide your details.
  2. We will reach out to schedule a site visit and plan your garden installation.
  3. Our volunteers will create the garden, and you'll get to enjoy a beautiful, low-maintenance garden that helps pollinators!

Get Started

Fill out the form below, and we will contact you to begin the process of sponsoring a garden. We look forward to working with you!
